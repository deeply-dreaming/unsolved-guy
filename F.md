F1: Gauss circle problem: How large can the error term h(r) be in the count of lattice points inside the circle of radius r, N(r)=πr^2+h(r)?
F2: Distinct distances on an n×n grid: What is the largest k of lattice points (x,y), 1≤x,y≤n, that can be chosen so that all mutual distances are distinct?
F3: No-four-on-a-circle / line coverings: Max points in [1,n]^2 with no four concyclic; also minimal t whose lines cover all n^2 points.
F4: No-three-in-line: Can 2n lattice points in an n×n grid be selected with no three collinear (and what are the best asymptotic bounds)?
F5: Quadratic residues (Schur’s conjecture): Is there an elementary proof that for primes p≡3 (mod 4) there are more quadratic residues in [1,(p-1)/2] than in the other half?
F6: Patterns of quadratic residues: For which (a,b) must every sufficiently large prime p admit r with r, r+a, r+b all quadratic residues mod p (i.e., when is O(a,b) finite)?
F7: Cubic Bhaskara analog: For p≡4 or 7 (mod 9), is x^3+py^3+p^2z^3-3pxyz=3 solvable iff w^3≡3 (mod p)?
F8: Quadratic residue difference sets: For primes p≡1 (mod 4), what is the maximum size of a set of quadratic residues whose pairwise differences are also quadratic residues?
F9: Primitive roots: For sufficiently large primes p, is there always a prime q<p such that q is a primitive root mod p?
F10: Residues of powers of two: For which integers k do there exist solutions to 2^n≡k (mod n), and do solutions exist for all k≠1?
F11: Factorial residues mod p: What is the distribution of 1!,2!,…,p! modulo p (e.g., which residue classes are missing, and how often do various patterns occur)?
F12: Parity of inverses mod p: For an odd prime p, how large is Np, the number of x with 0<x<p for which x and its inverse x^{-1} (mod p) have opposite parity?
F13: Covering systems of congruences: Do there exist covering systems with distinct moduli and arbitrarily large least modulus (and can an “odd-moduli” covering system exist)?
F14: Exact covering systems: Characterize disjoint covering congruence systems (each integer covered exactly once) and the constraints they impose on the moduli.
F15: Graham’s gcd-ratio problem: For 0<a1<…<an, must max_{i,j} ai/gcd(ai,aj) ≥ n (for all n, with explicit bounds)?
F16: Small-prime-power content in short intervals: With A(n,k)=∏_{p<k, p^a||n} p^a, how small can min_{1≤i≤k} A(n+i,k) be (e.g., is max_n min_{1≤i≤k} A(n+i,k)=o(k))?
F17: Zeta-series identities: Find/prove remarkable series and congruences connected to ζ(s) values (e.g., ζ(4) and Apéry-type phenomena for ζ(3)).
F18: Erdos-Szemeredi sum-product: For a set A of n numbers, how large must |(A+A) ∪ (A·A)| be (conjecturally > n^{2-ε})?
F19: Prime partitions max product: Can the max-product partition of n into distinct primes have far fewer parts than a max-cardinality partition (by an unbounded amount)?
F20: Zaremba’s conjecture: For every m>1, does some a coprime to m make a/m have all continued-fraction partial quotients ≤ B for an absolute constant B?
F21: 1-2 partial quotients: Is there c such that every n=a+b has a/b with all partial quotients ∈{1,2} and total sum of partial quotients ≤ c ln n?
F22: Algebraic continued fractions: For algebraic x of degree >2, can its simple continued fraction have bounded partial quotients, or must they be unbounded?
F23: Small differences between powers: How small can |3^n-2^m| be relative to 2^m (and related questions like approximating n! by powers of 2)?
F24: Decimal digit problems: Are there only finitely many nontrivial squares with exactly two distinct base-10 digits (and related zero-free/digit-sum questions)?
F25: Multiplicative persistence: Is persistence bounded in base 10 (and more generally, is there a universal bound d(b) for each base b)?
F26: Using only ones: Let f(n) be the least number of 1s needed to build n using + and ×; is f(n) ~ 3·log_3 n, and what are sharp bounds/identities for f(n)?
F27: Mahler’s generalized Farey series: Ordering quadratic roots by size, can consecutive-coefficient determinants be forced to stay in {0,±1} (or otherwise tightly bounded)?
F28: Determinant 1 with squared entries: Find integers a1,…,a9 (none 0 or ±1) such that a 3×3 determinant equals 1 and the determinant of the entrywise squares also equals 1.
F29: Two congruences, one always solvable: Find functions f(x), g(x) (mod p) such that for every n, at least one of f(x)≡n or g(x)≡n (mod p) is solvable.
F30: Distinct pair-sums from a polynomial: Find a polynomial P(x) such that all sums P(a)+P(b) (0≤a<b) are distinct.
F31: Digit-restricted quotients: In base 4 using digits {0,1,-1} (no 2), can every odd integer be written as a quotient of two such numbers (and what about base 3 analogs)?
F32: Reverse-and-add / RATS: Does reverse-and-add always reach a palindrome, and do base-10 RATS sequences always cycle or flow to Conway’s divergent pattern?
