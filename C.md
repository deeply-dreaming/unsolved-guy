C1: Goldbach's conjecture: Is every even integer > 4 expressible as the sum of two primes?
C2: Sums of consecutive primes: For f(n) = # representations of n as a sum of consecutive primes, is f(n) = 1 infinitely often (and does f(n) attain every k)?
C3: Lucky numbers: Do “lucky” analogs of prime conjectures hold (e.g., Goldbach-type representation counts for even n)?
C4: Ulam numbers: Do the U-numbers have positive density (and can there be arbitrarily large gaps / consecutive U-numbers)?
C5: Sums determining members of a set: For 2^k numbers, can more than two sets yield the same set of pairwise sums (for k > 3)?
C6: Addition chains (Scholz conjecture): Is l(2^n - 1) ≤ n - 1 + l(n) for all n?
C7: Frobenius coin problem: For coprime a1,…,an, find g(a1,…,an), the largest integer not representable as ∑ a_i x_i (x_i≥0), especially for n≥3.
C8: Distinct subset sums: Maximize m integers ≤ 2^k whose subset sums are all distinct (is m = k + O(1))?
C9: Sidon sequences / packing pair sums: Is the maximum size m of a Sidon set in [1, n] equal to n^{1/2} + O(1) (Erdos-Turan conjecture)?
C10: Perfect difference sets: Do they exist only for prime-power parameters, and can any distinct-difference set be extended to a perfect one?
C11: B_h sequences (h = 3): Is liminf A_h(n)/n^{1/h} = 0 for odd h (in particular h = 3)?
C12: Postage stamp problem: For given h and k, maximize the h-range n(h, k) of a k-element additive basis of order h.
C13: Modular covering / harmonious labelling: Are all trees harmonious (and graceful), and how large can a harmonious graph on v vertices be?
C14: Maximal sum-free sets: Given n distinct integers, how large a subset can always be chosen with no relation a_i + a_j = a_k?
C15: Maximal zero-sum-free sets: For modulus m, what is the largest k(m) such that one can choose k(m) distinct residues with no nonempty subset summing to 0 (mod m)?
C16: Nonaveraging sets: What is the maximal size f(x) of a subset of [0, x] containing no element that is the arithmetic mean of others?
C17: Minimum overlap problem: For A ⊂ {1, …, 2n} of size n, is M(n) (the minimal possible max_k #{(a,b): a∈A, b∉A, a-b=k}) asymptotic to c·n?
C18: n queens problem (domination): What is the minimum number of queens needed to cover (occupy or attack) every square of an n×n chessboard?
C19: Independent sequences: Is every weakly independent sequence a finite union of strongly independent sequences?
C20: Sums of squares: Characterize n representable as a sum of four pairwise coprime squares (and related coprime-square representation problems).
C21: Sums of higher powers: For k ≥ 2, what is s_k, the largest integer not representable as a sum of distinct k-th powers?
